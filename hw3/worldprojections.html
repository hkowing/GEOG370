WGS84 Projection

This Mercator map is one of the most common types of world maps. It uses the cylindrical projection method. The Mercator projection preserves distance very well, which is why it was used primarily for navigational purposes. It also preserves shape relatively well, but is distorted the closer you get to the poles. It significantly distorts both area and distance.

Aitoff Projection

Based on its equal-area property, the Aitoff projection is best when one needs to maintain accurate area relationships.
