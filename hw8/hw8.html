Geoprocessing Analysis: Answering a Real-Life Question Using Geoprocessing Tools

In this map, I wanted to answer the question, "How many of Madison's Metro bus stops and their surrounding areas are located in nationally-registered Historic Areas?" To answer this quesiton, I first got bus stops and historic areas data sets from Madison's public data sets. I then used the "Dissolve" geoprocessing tool on the historic areas polygons so they act as one field. Because I wanted to visualize the areas that each bus stop serves, I created 150 meter buffers around them. Finally, I used the "Intersection" geoprocessing tool on both the bus stops and their buffers to determine which of them coincide with the historic areas polygon. Using different colors on these geoprocessed vectors, I then visualized the result of my question.
